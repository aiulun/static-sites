Our Story

The story of Elegance

From then to now

Elegance was founded in 2010 by renowned hairstylist, Lisa Johnson. With over 20 years of experience in the industry, Lisa's vision was to create a salon that offered clients a luxurious and personalized experience. Elegance quickly became a sought-after destination for those seeking expert hair services in a relaxing and welcoming atmosphere. Lisa's team of skilled professionals are dedicated to staying up-to-date with the latest trends and techniques in the industry, while also providing exceptional customer service. With a focus on creating customized looks that enhance each client's individual beauty, Elegance has become a staple in the community and beyond.
