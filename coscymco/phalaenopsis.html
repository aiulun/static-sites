Potted Phalaenopsis Orchids

$15.00-$25.00 each (tax inc.)

Our Phalaenopsis orchids are produced either via seed or by division propagation. Our hybrid Phalaenopsis from seed are bred with parents that have strong growth and bloom habits year round, branching capabilities of the flower stems and producing flower stems that will produce blooms 3-4 months with the proper care. We highly suggest growing these orchids indoors year round, water about once a week allowing the soil to start to dry before each watering, and USE FERTILIZER (in all our years of retailing and consulting with orchids and plants in general, fertilizer is usually the most over looked factor that will greatly optimize the plants performance).
