Assorted Succulents

2" - $1 each (tax inc.)

4" - $4.00 or 3/$10 each (tax inc.)

6" - $7.00 or 2/$12 each (tax inc.)

The reason we are able to sell our 2” succulents at $1 each is because we do not source them out from another grower, all of our cuttings are grown, produced, processed, planted and rooted at our nursery from the cutting stage all the way up to final sale to the consumer. The downfall to solely producing our own succulents is sometimes we are limited from having the latest varieties. We ensure that all of our plants sold are well rooted, put through our pest management program to protect being attacked by unwanted pest while not affecting beneficial ones, hardened off to the proper amount of light to help promote color and avoiding any blemishing from sunburning. Our succulents are divided into two areas, the outdoor and indoor varieties. The outdoor varieties will typically want a finally location that receives at least 2-3 hours of direct sunlight each day to maintain their red coloring, which essentially is like their version of having a tan. Conversely, the indoor succulents want final destination that receives bright, but filtered sunlight. We grow well over 50 different varieties of succulents and the selection is always changing depending on what’s in rotation.
